Tile World Clock — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-07

1. Overview

Tile World Clock is designed to keep your data on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information to our servers or third parties.

2. Data Stored Locally

The following information is saved locally on your device so that the app can function as expected:

  • Selected cities, layout preferences, and display options
  • Alarm configurations (labels, schedule, repeat, duration)
  • General settings such as language override, opacity, wallpaper path
  • Cached weather and holiday results retrieved from public APIs

All local data resides under the standard application data directory (for Windows, %APPDATA%\Tile World Clock). Removing the application will remove these files.

3. Network Requests

The app makes outbound requests to trusted public services in order to populate informational widgets:

  • Open-Meteo API — retrieves current weather data for selected cities.
  • Nager.Date API — fetches public holiday information for the chosen country.

Requests are sent directly from your device to these providers; we do not proxy or log them. No personal identifiers are attached.

4. Licensing

When Tile World Clock is distributed through the Microsoft Store, it invokes a helper component (LicenseBridge.exe) to confirm trial or purchase status. This helper communicates solely with Microsoft Store licensing services.
